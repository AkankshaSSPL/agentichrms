# Employee Leave Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy outlines the leave entitlements for all employees of the organization. It applies to full-time and part-time employees who have completed their probation period.

## 2. Annual Leave (Vacation)

### 2.1 Entitlement
- Full-time employees: 20 working days per calendar year
- Part-time employees: Pro-rated based on hours worked
- New employees: Pro-rated for the first year of employment

### 2.2 Carry Forward
- Maximum 5 days can be carried forward to the next year
- Carried forward leave must be used by March 31st
- Unused leave beyond the limit will be forfeited

### 2.3 Application Process
1. Submit leave request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approval is required for all leave requests
3. Peak periods (December, Summer) require 4 weeks advance notice

## 3. Sick Leave

### 3.1 Entitlement
- All employees: 10 days paid sick leave per year
- Sick leave does not carry forward

### 3.2 Notification
- Notify your manager before your shift starts or within 1 hour
- Medical certificate required for absences of 3+ consecutive days

### 3.3 Extended Illness
- Absences exceeding 10 days may be covered under Short-Term Disability
- Contact HR for STD application process

## 4. Personal/Emergency Leave
- 3 days per year for personal matters
- May be used for:
  - Family emergencies
  - Religious observances
  - Personal appointments that cannot be scheduled outside work hours

## 5. Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family (spouse, children, parents): 5 days
- Extended family (siblings, grandparents): 3 days
- Close friends or other relatives: 1 day (at manager discretion)

## 6. Parental Leave

### 6.1 Maternity Leave
- 16 weeks paid leave at 100% salary
- Additional 10 weeks unpaid leave available

### 6.2 Paternity Leave
- 4 weeks paid leave at 100% salary
- Must be taken within 6 months of birth/adoption

### 6.3 Adoption Leave
- Same entitlements as maternity/paternity leave
- Begins on the day of custody

## 7. Public Holidays
The company observes the following paid holidays:
1. New Year's Day
2. Republic Day (India) / Independence Day (USA)
3. Holi / Easter Monday
4. Labor Day
5. Independence Day (India) / Veterans Day (USA)
6. Diwali / Thanksgiving
7. Christmas Day
8. 2 Floating holidays (employee choice)

## 8. Leave Without Pay (LWP)
- May be granted at management discretion
- Maximum 30 days per year
- Benefits may be affected during LWP period

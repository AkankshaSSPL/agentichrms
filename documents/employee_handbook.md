# Employee Handbook

## 1. Code of Conduct
We expect integrity, respect, and professionalism.
- **Respect**: Treat colleagues with dignity. Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.
- **Confidentiality**: Protect company and client data. Do not share credentials.
- **Social Media**: Be responsible when mentioning the company online.

## 2. Working Hours
- **Standard Hours**: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
- **Flexibility**: We focus on output. Flexible start/end times are allowed with manager agreement, provided core hours (10-4) are overlapped.
- **Break**: 1 hour for lunch.

## 3. Performance Review
- **Cycle**: Twice a year (Mid-year in October, Annual in April).
- **Process**: Self-appraisal -> Manager Review -> Calibration -> Feedback discussion.
- **Rating Scale**: 1 (Improvement Needed) to 5 (Outstanding).

## 4. Career Growth
- **Promotions**: Evaluated during the Annual review cycle.
- **internal Mobility**: Employees can apply for internal open positions after completing 12 months in current role.
- **L&D**: Learning budget of $500/year per employee for courses and certifications.

## 5. Grievance Redressal
If you face any issues:
1. Talk to your immediate manager.
2. If unresolved or sensitive, contact the HR Business Partner.
3. For harassment complaints, email the Internal Committee (IC) at ic@company.com immediately.

## 6. Travel Policy
- **Approval**: Travel request must be approved by Dept Head.
- **Per Diem**: $50/day for domestic, $100/day for international travel (for food/incidentals).
- **Booking**: flight/hotel bookings via the corporate travel portal.

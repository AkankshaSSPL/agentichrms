Title: Company Leave Policy

1. Annual Leave
   - Employees are entitled to 20 working days of paid annual leave per year.
   - Leave accrues monthly and can be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days.

2. Sick Leave
   - Employees may take up to 12 paid sick days per calendar year.
   - A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

3. Casual Leave
   - 6 casual leave days per year, not cumulative.
   - Must be approved at least 2 days in advance unless in emergency.

4. Maternity/Paternity Leave
   - Female employees: 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
   - Male employees: 15 days of paid paternity leave.

5. Notice for Leave
   - All non-emergency leaves must be requested at least 7 days in advance.

Title: Employee Code of Conduct

1. Professionalism
   - Maintain respectful communication and decorum in all interactions.
   - No discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or disability.

2. Confidentiality
   - Protect company IP, financials, and client information.
   - Any breach will result in disciplinary action.

3. Attendance & Punctuality
   - Employees must log in by 10:00 AM IST.
   - Repeated tardiness will be flagged during performance review.

4. Social Media
   - Avoid sharing confidential or defamatory content about the company.
   - Do not represent the company without permission.

5. Grievances
   - All grievances should be reported to HR via official channel.
   - Anonymous reports can be submitted via ethics@company.com.

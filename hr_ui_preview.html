What is moonlighting disclosure?
✦ Answer

The moonlighting disclosure policy requires employees to inform the company about any consulting work, directorship, or similar relationships with other persons or entities during their employment.


This is to ensure transparency and manage potential conflicts of interest. Key obligations include:

  • Disclosure: Notify HR of any external work or engagements.
  • Approval: Certain roles may require prior written approval.
  • Conflict of Interest: Engagements that compete with company interests are prohibited.

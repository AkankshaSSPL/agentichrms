# Onboarding Guide for New Joiners

## 1. Welcome!
Welcome to the team! This guide outlines the steps for a smooth onboarding experience during your first week.

## 2. Pre-boarding (Before Day 1)
- **Offer Acceptance**: Sign and return the digital offer letter.
- **Background Check**: Submit documents for background verification (ID proof, address proof, education certificates).
- **Laptop Dispatch**: IT team will courier your laptop and welcome kit to your address.

## 3. Day 1 Checklist
- **9:30 AM**: Login to your corporate email (credentials sent to personal email).
- **10:00 AM**: HR Induction session (Google Meet link in calendar).
- **11:00 AM**: IT Setup session (VPN, Slack, Jira, VS Code installation).
- **2:00 PM**: Team Introduction meeting.

## 4. Mandatory Training
All new joiners must complete the following within the first week:
1. **Code of Conduct**: Read and digitally sign.
2. **InfoSec Awareness**: 30-minute video + quiz.
3. **Anti-Harassment (POSH)**: 45-minute interactive module.
4. **Product Overview**: Recorded sessions on company products.

## 5. Buddy System
You will be assigned a "Buddy" from your team. Your buddy is your go-to person for informal questions about culture, tools, and processes. They will schedule a lunch/coffee chat with you.

## 6. Probation
- Startups probation period is 3 months.
- A review will be conducted at 45 days and 90 days.
- Goals for probation will be set by your manager in the first week.

## 7. Support
- **HR Queries**: email hr-support@company.com
- **IT Issues**: Raise a ticket on the IT Helpdesk portal or message #it-help on Slack.
- **Payroll**: email finance@company.com

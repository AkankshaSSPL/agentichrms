# Remote Work & Work From Home Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for employees who work remotely or from home. It ensures productivity, collaboration, and work-life balance while maintaining company standards.

## 2. Eligibility

### 2.1 Eligible Roles
- Software Development
- Design
- Marketing
- Customer Support (with VPN access)
- Sales (field positions)
- Project Management

### 2.2 Ineligible Roles
- Reception/Front Desk
- Facilities Management
- Hardware/IT Support (on-site)
- Security Personnel

### 2.3 Requirements
- Minimum 6 months of employment
- Performance rating of "Meets Expectations" or higher
- Manager approval
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)

## 3. Work From Home Options

### 3.1 Hybrid Schedule
- 3 days in office, 2 days remote per week
- Core office days: Tuesday and Thursday
- Remote days chosen by employee with manager approval

### 3.2 Full Remote
- Available for select positions
- Requires Director-level approval
- Quarterly in-person meetings required

### 3.3 Occasional WFH
- Up to 2 days per month
- 24-hour advance notice required
- No approval needed (just notification)

## 4. Equipment & Setup

### 4.1 Company-Provided Equipment
- Laptop with required software
- VPN access
- Collaboration tools (Slack, Zoom, etc.)
- Optional: Monitor upon request (one-time)

### 4.2 Employee Responsibility
- Ergonomic workspace
- Reliable internet connection
- Quiet environment for calls
- Secure password-protected WiFi

### 4.3 Expense Reimbursement
- Internet allowance: ₹500/month (or $30/month)
- Home office stipend: One-time ₹10,000 (or $200)
- Submit receipts through expense portal

## 5. Working Hours & Availability

### 5.1 Core Hours
- All remote employees must be available: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM IST
- Flexibility outside core hours for personal schedules

### 5.2 Response Time
- Slack/Email: Within 2 hours during work hours
- Urgent matters: Immediate response expected

### 5.3 Video Calls
- Camera ON for team meetings and 1:1s
- Professional background or virtual background required

## 6. Security & Confidentiality

### 6.1 Data Protection
- Use company VPN for all work activities
- No public WiFi for accessing company systems
- Screen lock when away from device

### 6.2 Physical Security
- Work documents must be stored securely
- No printing of confidential materials at home
- Shred any printed documents

### 6.3 Password Requirements
- Strong passwords for all accounts
- Two-factor authentication mandatory
- Password manager recommended

## 7. Communication

### 7.1 Daily Check-ins
- Post daily standup in team Slack channel by 10:30 AM
- Format: What I did yesterday, What I'm doing today, Blockers

### 7.2 Meetings
- All meetings should have video option
- Meeting notes must be shared within 24 hours
- Time zones should be considered for global teams

## 8. Performance Expectations
- Same performance standards as office work
- Regular 1:1 meetings with manager (weekly)
- Deliverables and deadlines remain unchanged
- Abuse of WFH privilege may result in revocation

## 9. Health & Wellbeing
- Take regular breaks (Pomodoro technique encouraged)
- Maintain work-life boundaries
- Use mental health resources available through EAP
- Report any ergonomic issues to HR

## 10. Policy Violations
First offense: Written warning
Second offense: WFH privilege suspension (3 months)
Third offense: Permanent removal from remote work program

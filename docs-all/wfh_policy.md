# Work From Home (WFH) Policy

## 1. Overview
The company supports flexible working arrangements, including the option to work from home, to improve work-life balance and productivity.

## 2. Eligibility
- All full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible.
- Roles requiring physical presence (e.g., security, facilities, hardware lab access) may be exempt or restricted.

## 3. Allowance
- **Hybrid Model**: Employees may work from home up to **2 days per week**.
- **Core Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday are core office days where presence is encouraged for collaboration.
- **Ad-hoc WFH**: Additional days may be granted by the manager for specific personal reasons or emergencies.

## 4. Guidelines
- **Availability**: Employees must be available on Slack/Teams and phone during core working hours (10:00 AM - 6:00 PM).
- **Meetings**: Video must be enabled for team meetings and client calls.
- **Equipment**: Company provides a laptop. Employees must ensure they have a stable high-speed internet connection at home.
- **Security**: Do not use public Wi-Fi without VPN. Ensure screen privacy.

## 5. Procedure
- Mark your status as "Remote" on the internal calendar/Slack before 9:30 AM.
- No formal approval is needed for the standard 2 days, but inform your team in standup.
- Long-term remote work (more than 1 week) requires Director-level approval.

## 6. Exceptions
- During severe weather alerts or pandemics, the company may declare mandatory WFH for all.
- New joiners are required to work from office for the first 2 weeks for onboarding.
